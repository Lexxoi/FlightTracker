## ✈️ FlightTracker

Records flight times and costs and provides detailed flight statistics.

---

## ✨ Features

### ⏱ Flight Tracking

* **Flight Time Tracking** – Records your flight times and shows them in Flight Master tooltips.
* **In-Flight Timer** – Displays the remaining time left during a flight.
* **Flight Time Tooltips** – Shows known flight times in Flight Master menu tooltips.

### ⚙️ Automation

* **Auto Dismount** – Automatically dismounts you when speaking to a Flight Master.
* **Confirmation Window** – Displays a confirmation window before accepting a flight.

### 👥 Social

* **Party/Raid Announcement** – Automatically share your ETA with your group.

### 📊 Statistics

* Track your:

  * Total flights taken
  * Total gold spent
  * Total time flown
  * Longest flight

### 📍 Completion

* **Route Checklist** – A checklist for every possible flight route available to your faction.

### 🖥 Commands

* `/ft` or `/flighttracker` — Open the addon UI.
